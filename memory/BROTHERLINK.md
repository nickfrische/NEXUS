*Last updated: 2026-04-16. Source: Nick's direct context dump.*

## THE PRODUCT

BrotherLink is a chapter management and alumni engagement platform for fraternities, built by BPN Solutions.

**Live deployment:** Phi Gamma Delta (FIJI) Zeta Chapter at IU — the proof-of-concept anchor for all other sales.

### What's In It

**For graduate brothers:**
- Member/alumni directory
- Career and opportunity board
- Pledge class and profile pages
- Chapter news feeds
- Giving and philanthropy

**For actives:**
- Events calendar with RSVP
- Messaging and group communication
- Document vault (bylaws, NME materials, award applications)
- Officer admin portal with analytics
- Media sharing
- Chapter history

**Platform:** Web + mobile (iOS + Android), one login.

**Tech stack:** React Native, Supabase (auth/backend), Heroku.

**Domain:** brotherlink.ai (migrated from Vercel subdomain)

## BRAND & POSITIONING

**Metaphor:** A chain — linking brothers across generations, pledge classes, cities, and careers.

Active chapters are duct-taping together 5+ apps for events, dues, docs, comms — and every officer transition starts from scratch.

## WORKING PRINCIPLES (Nick's established rules)

1. **Problem-first, always.** Never feature lists. Center on alumni disconnection, lost donations, untapped talent pipelines.
2. **Discovery before pitch.** FIJI Zeta's proof sequences all other closes. Don't try to close anyone until FIJI is a case study.
3. **Never anchor price before structure is set.** Learned expensively.
4. **Value-based pricing over fairness.** Tiers justified by scope and buyer, not internal fairness comparisons.
5. **Slides locked means script compensates.** Emotional framing, setup, and context live in delivery.
