## FULL SCRIPT (5 min)

### Hook (30 sec)
A Cushman & Wakefield broker managing a $500 million book of business told us last month — and I'm quoting — "I've been thinking about building this for six years. Nothing on the market actually works for how we operate."

A private equity firm replacing a whiteboard to track 200 trailers found out that every GPS platform on the market wanted to lock them into a per-unit subscription and keep their data.

These are not small companies. These are established, successful businesses. And they are stuck.

### Problem (45 sec)
Here's the gap nobody's solving.

Generic SaaS — HubSpot, Salesforce, off-the-shelf tools — was built for the average company. It doesn't fit how operators actually work.

Enterprise custom software costs $500K and takes two years.

So what do mid-market businesses do? They patch it together. Spreadsheets. Whiteboards. Offshore teams in India doing what software should be doing. Disconnected systems that don't talk to each other.

The result: time wasted, money left on the table, and zero real-time visibility into their own business.

### Solution (45 sec)
BPN Solutions builds the custom digital execution layer that closes that gap.

Not off-the-shelf. Not enterprise-priced. We come into your business, understand exactly how you operate, and build the software and AI automation around your actual workflow — in weeks, not years.

Your data stays yours. Your systems work together. And your team does in two hours what used to take twenty.

### Traction (75 sec)
We're not pitching a concept. This is already happening.

Indiana state government validated our demand — 16 businesses used state grant funding to hire BPN as their software vendor through the INTAP program.

DEEM: a logistics company with 200+ trailers replaced a whiteboard with a real-time GPS tracking platform we built. Their data, their system, no per-unit lock-in.

High School Hustle: a multi-campus SaaS platform we built now serves 44 campuses and 2,000+ students, driving nearly $1 million in revenue for our client.

Cushman & Wakefield: we're in active scoping with brokers at one of the largest commercial real estate firms in the world. They told us — nobody has built what they actually need. BPN is building it.

And this morning, a private equity firm verbally committed to their first automation project with us — replacing the offshore team they were paying every month to do what AI can now do in seconds.

### Market (30 sec)
Every mid-market business in America lives in this gap. Too complex for generic software. Too small for enterprise custom builds.

We're starting in Indiana — because Indiana business is relationship-driven and we have roots here. But the problem is everywhere. The lower-middle market is a $50 billion opportunity that enterprise software companies have ignored.

### Team (20 sec)
Three IU FIJI pledge brothers. Nick Frische leads business development. Blake Marcotte builds the product. Philip Hess owns our tracking vertical. We've been building together since freshman year and we're all in.

### Ask (20 sec)
We're closing recurring revenue clients this week. We're looking for advisors who've scaled mid-market software businesses or have PE backgrounds. And we're looking for any connections into established Indiana businesses that are still running on spreadsheets and whiteboards.

If that's you or someone you know — come find me after.
