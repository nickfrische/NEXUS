## Background

Olivia holds a B.S. and M.P.A. from IU's O'Neill School of Public and Environmental Affairs, and an MBA from Romain College of Business at the University of Southern Indiana. She is an IU alum and Bloomington native.

Before IU Ventures, she was Director of Operations at an Arizona-based business accelerator focused on early-stage healthcare and life science companies in the Greater Phoenix area.

She joined IU Ventures in September 2024 as Senior Analyst and was promoted to Executive Director of the IU Angel Network within approximately 18 months -- a significant jump. She drove an 81% increase in month-over-month deal flow in Q4-2024 through aggressive deal sourcing, network leveraging, and direct outreach.

Her own words on what drew her to VC: "Venture capital plays a huge role in giving companies the runway they need to turn innovative ideas into real-world solutions. That's part of what drew me into the VC side -- seeing the transformative impact funding can have on startups and wanting to be part of that process."

On founders: "I love working with early-stage entrepreneurs. They aren't just building businesses -- they're tackling real-world problems. Being part of their journey, watching their ideas grow from a spark of inspiration into something tangible, is immensely rewarding."
