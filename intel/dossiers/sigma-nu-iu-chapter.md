## KEY PAIN POINTS

1. **Roster chaos** — Four different rosters (Sigma Nu national, IU, composites, etc.) with no match between them
2. **Officer turnover** — Institutional knowledge resets every year with each leadership transition
3. **Alumni chair burnout** — One person doing all alumni comms manually with no infrastructure
4. **Alumni leverage gap** — Active members can't tap alumni for jobs/internships due to the age gap in the network
5. **Low newsletter performance** — Donations trickle in, nothing is tracked well
6. **No centralized alumni database** — No single source of truth accessible to the house
