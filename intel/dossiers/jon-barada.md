## Background

Jon grew up in Rushville, Indiana (small town, Indiana roots). Holds a BS from Kelley School of Business (IU) and an MBA from Butler University.

From 2013 to 2020, he was President and CEO of the Bloomington Health Foundation, overseeing a $35M endowed fund focused on community health. During his tenure he secured a $675,000 national grant from the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation to build healthcare policymaking infrastructure for underserved communities, led fundraising for the expansion of IU Health Bloomington Hospital's NICU, Hospice House, and Trauma Department, and coordinated the 20th anniversary of Hoosiers Outrun Cancer. He left in fall 2020 but stayed on the board -- a sign of how he operates relationships.

He joined IU Ventures in January 2021 as VP of Engagement. His mandate: activate and cultivate the global IU Founders and Funders Network, a nearly 800,000-person alumni base. He manages alumni and student engagement, marketing, fundraising, and angel network development.
