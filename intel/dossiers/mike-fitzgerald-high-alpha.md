## The Opportunity

**AI Due Diligence for Venture Portfolio Companies**

High Alpha's core problem: they've invested in dozens of SaaS companies. AI is disrupting the SaaS model. They need to know:
1. How defensible is each portfolio company's moat against AI?
2. Where can AI add value inside the product?
3. Is the code quality solid or is this held together with duct tape?

BPN's angle: come in as the technical AI layer that High Alpha doesn't have in-house. Not business strategy (they handle that) — specifically the AI disruption assessment and implementation capability.

**The right framing for High Alpha (SaaS-specific, NOT traditional business):**
- AI disruption risk: how quickly could this software be rebuilt by AI? What's the real moat?
- Code quality / defensibility: is the underlying software sound? Was it built to last?
- AI value creation: where within the product/stack can AI meaningfully improve it?
- Build capability: BPN can implement improvements if the portfolio company lacks devs

**What NOT to lead with for High Alpha:**
- Process automation for traditional business workflows (wrong audience — these are SaaS founders)
- Business strategy consulting (High Alpha already does this)
