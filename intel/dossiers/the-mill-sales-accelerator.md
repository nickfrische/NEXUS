## Moti's Core Framework

> "Treat sales like an engineered system, not random art."

Key pillars:
1. **Clarify your ideal client** — know exactly who you help and how
2. **Develop a "helping statement"** — crisp, non-pitchy positioning
3. **Block consistent time** for proactive sales activity (not reactive)
4. **Use a simple, repeatable sales process** with a visible pipeline
5. **The 1–4 Maturity Scale:** Most founders are at 1–2 (referrals only, ad-hoc outreach, little structure). Program targets getting to 3–4.
